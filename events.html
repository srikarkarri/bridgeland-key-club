Where we show up

Featured projects.

Our most consistent service partnerships. The places we keep coming back to because the work matters.

Cypress, TX

Community of Faith

Club members serve through Community of Faith's monthly Serve Saturday projects — everything from food distributions to the Boys & Girls Country Spring Carnival, bringing practical help and hope to families around Cypress and Hockley.

Food distribution Community events
Houston Food Bank Partner

MESA-Outreach, Inc.

A Houston Food Bank partner, MESA's pantry distributes emergency groceries to food-insecure neighbors, while its "Books to Kids," school-supply, and scholarship programs invest in children's futures. We help stock shelves, hand out food, and read with kids during literacy events.

Food pantry Literacy
Cy-Hope

Hope Chest Resale Market

The Hope Chest's 25,000 sq ft resale warehouse turns gently-used treasures into funding for Cy-Hope's youth programs. Our volunteers sort donations and staff sales so every purchase helps underserved Cy-Fair students thrive.
