What we do

Three things, on repeat,
for nine years now.

Key Club International boils down to one idea: caring is our way of life. At Bridgeland we live that out through service, leadership, and a community that actually shows up for each other.

01 · Service

Show up where it matters

Park cleanups, food bank shifts, elementary tutoring, blood drives. We run 18+ events a semester with partners across Cypress.

02 · Leadership

Real responsibility, not a title

Project leads run their own events end-to-end: budget, volunteers, day-of logistics. You'll learn to lead by leading.

03 · Friendship

Find your people, fast

Socials every month, divisional events, district convention in Dallas. The friend group that comes with the service is the secret prize.
