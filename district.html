About Division 3W

Part of the Texas-Oklahoma District

Division 3W covers clubs in the Greater Houston area. As a division we hold regular divisional council meetings (DCM) and co-host service projects across member clubs.

Annual · Winter

LEDCON 2026.

Leadership & Development Conference

LEDCON is the Texas-Oklahoma District's premier leadership training event, bringing together Key Club officers from across the district for a full weekend of workshops, skill-building, and collaboration. Club officers attend sessions on project management, public speaking, running effective meetings, and community impact.

LEDCON 2026 has concluded. Results are now available. See the contest winners and scholarship recipients from this year's conference.

LEDCON 2026 page ↗ Contest & scholarship winners ↗
What happens at LEDCON
  • Leadership workshops led by district officers
  • Service project competitions & showcases
  • Scholarship awards ceremony
  • Networking with Key Clubbers across TX-OK
  • Spirit events & division camaraderie
Who should attend

Club officers, committee chairs, and any member interested in stepping into a leadership role. Attendance is highly encouraged before running for a district or division position.
