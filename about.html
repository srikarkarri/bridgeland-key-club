What we are · what we do

A student-led service organization, since 2017.

Bridgeland Key Club is the BHS chapter of Key Club International, the largest high-school service organization in the world. We're chartered through the Cypress Kiwanis Club and represent Division 3W of the Texas-Oklahoma District.

Our mission

Empower student members to lead by serving.

We partner with local nonprofits, elementary schools, and community leaders to make a real difference in Cypress. Members run the projects: choosing where we show up, leading the day-of logistics, and signing the recap reports themselves. The leadership skill comes from the leadership work.

What is Key Club, really?

The 30-second version.

International

Key Club International

Founded in 1925. Today: 240,000+ members across 5,000+ clubs in 38 countries. The largest student-led service organization on the planet.

District

Texas-Oklahoma District

Our district covers all of Texas and Oklahoma, with 240+ clubs and 14,000+ members. We elect district officers each year at convention in Dallas.

Local

Cypress Kiwanis sponsorship

Every Key Club is sponsored by a local Kiwanis Club. Ours is Cypress Kiwanis. They fund our charter and give us advisors who've been in service for decades.

The Objects of Key Club International
  1. 1
    To develop initiative and leadership.
  2. 2
    To provide experience in living and working together.
  3. 3
    To serve the school and community.
  4. 4
    To cooperate with the school principal.
  5. 5
    To prepare for useful citizenship.
  6. 6
    To accept and promote the following ideals:
    I Give primacy to the human and spiritual rather than to the material values of life.
    II Encourage the daily living of the Golden Rule in all human relationships.
    III Promote higher standards in scholarship, sportsmanship, and social contacts.
    IV Develop, by precept and example, a more intelligent, aggressive, and serviceable citizenship.
    V Provide a practical means to form enduring friendships, render altruistic service, and build better communities.
    VI Cooperate in creating and maintaining the sound public opinion and high idealism which make possible righteousness, justice, patriotism, and good will.
